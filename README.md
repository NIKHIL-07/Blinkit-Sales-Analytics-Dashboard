# 🛒 Blinkit Retail Sales Analysis & Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project features an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the sales performance, outlet characteristics, and product inventory of Blinkit (India's Last Minute App). The objective of this analysis is to understand purchasing patterns, evaluate outlet efficiency, and identify key drivers for revenue generation to help optimize future business strategies.

## 💡 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** $1.20M
* **Average Sales:** $141
* **Number of Items:** 8,523
* **Average Rating:** 3.9 ⭐

## 📈 Key Insights
1. **Sales by Fat Content:** Low-Fat products are the primary driver of revenue, generating $776.32K compared to $425.36K from Regular products.
2. **Item Category Performance:** 'Fruits and Vegetables' and 'Snack Foods' are the highest-grossing categories, each generating approximately $0.18M in sales.
3. **Geographic & Size Impact:** 
   * **Tier 3** locations generate the highest total revenue ($472.13K).
   * **Medium-sized** outlets contribute the most to total sales ($507.90K) compared to Small and High-capacity outlets.
4. **Outlet Type Analysis:** Supermarket Type 1 dominates the revenue stream, bringing in $787.55K with 5,577 items sold.
5. **Establishment Trend:** The line chart tracking outlet establishment over time reveals historical peaks and a notable dip around 2010, showing recovery and stabilization in subsequent years.

## 📝 Dataset Description
The analysis is driven by the `BlinkIT Grocery Data.csv` dataset, which contains **8,523 records** and **12 features** representing individual product sales across various Blinkit outlet locations.

**Data Dictionary:**
* **Item Identifier:** Unique product identification code.
* **Item Fat Content:** Indicates whether the product is "Low Fat" or "Regular".
* **Item Type:** The broad category the product belongs to (e.g., Dairy, Soft Drinks, Snack Foods).
* **Item Weight:** The physical weight of the product.
* **Item Visibility:** The percentage of total display area allocated to this product in the store.
* **Outlet Identifier:** Unique store identification code.
* **Outlet Establishment Year:** The year the specific outlet was opened (ranging from 2000 to 2022).
* **Outlet Size:** The physical capacity of the store (Small, Medium, High).
* **Outlet Location Type:** The geographical tier classification of the city (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3).
* **Outlet Type:** The operational format of the store (Supermarket Type 1, Supermarket Type 2, Grocery Store).
* **Rating:** The average customer rating given to the item (out of 5.0).
* **Total Sales:** *(Target Variable)* Total revenue generated by the item at that specific outlet.
